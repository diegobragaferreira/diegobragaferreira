Help

"Clear" resets the scene and erase all tensors and links. "Grid" change between {none, square, diamond, isometrical} grids and snaps the tensors in it. "Draw" button selects draw mode: 1 click in blank creates a tensor, 1 click in tensor selects it until another tensor (or point in blank) is selected to then be connected, 2 clicks erases a tensor or point. "Move" button enables the indexing and selected tensors or points can be moved freely. "Save" button saves the state of the diagram, and put it in "download" link to be downloaded. This is a "working in progress" and feedback is appreciated (contact@diegoferreira.net).
