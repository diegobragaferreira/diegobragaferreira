Instructions

  • Draw Mode: Click on blank space to create a tensor. Click a tensor to select it, then click another to connect.
  • Erase: Double-click a tensor or point to remove it.
  • Move Mode: Enables dragging tensors freely.
  • Grid: Change grid snapping (Square, Diamond, Isometric).
This is a work in progress. Feedback is appreciated at contact@diegoferreira.net.
